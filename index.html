Why this archive exists

On the night of 31 May 1981, the Jaffna Public Library was set ablaze. More than 97,000 books, palm-leaf manuscripts and 19th-century newspapers turned to ash — among the largest acts of cultural destruction in South Asia.

TEL Library was founded on the 40th anniversary of that loss. We cannot recover what burned — but we can make sure it is never lost again. Everything here is gathered, digitised and kept by volunteers across the diaspora.
